OONI, or the Open Observatory of Network Interference, is a global community driven network of probes that measure the internet to identify "network anomalies" that can be a sign of internet censorship or surveillance. In the map bellow you can see where measurements are run the most and in which countries we have identified, through our measurements, instances of internet censorship and particular types of censorship software or vendors.
